Understanding the Metrics
Accuracy

Percentage of correct predictions out of all predictions made. Higher is better.

Precision

Of all predicted positive cases, how many were actually positive. Important to minimize false alarms.

Recall (Sensitivity)

Of all actual positive cases, how many were correctly identified. Critical in medical diagnosis to avoid missing cases.

F1 Score

Harmonic mean of precision and recall. Balances both metrics.

ROC-AUC Score

Area Under the ROC Curve. Measures model's ability to distinguish between classes. 1.0 is perfect, 0.5 is random. Generally, >0.8 is considered good.
